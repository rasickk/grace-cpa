Who We Are

Grace CPA, PSC is a full-service certified public accounting firm headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky. We serve individuals, small businesses, and mid-sized companies across a wide range of industries, providing tailored accounting, tax, and financial consulting services that help our clients achieve their goals.

From our founding, we have believed that great accounting is more than numbers — it's about understanding each client's unique situation and delivering solutions that make a real difference. Our team brings deep technical expertise and a genuine commitment to client success to every engagement.

Whether you're a startup looking for guidance on business formation, an established business needing reliable bookkeeping, or an individual seeking to minimize your tax burden, Grace CPA is here to help you move forward with confidence.
