About Grace CPA, PSC

A trusted CPA firm built on listening, integrity, and results.

Who We Are

Grace CPA, PSC is a certified public accounting firm based in Louisville, Kentucky. We serve individuals, small businesses, and established enterprises throughout the Louisville metro area and beyond.

At the heart of everything we do is a simple belief: listening is the foundation of great service. We take the time to truly understand our clients — their goals, their challenges, and their vision for the future — so we can deliver accounting and tax solutions that actually make a difference.

From startups and entrepreneurs to established enterprises, we provide quality accounting services and strategic tax planning to all types of businesses. We also offer prompt and accurate income tax preparation for business owners and individuals.
