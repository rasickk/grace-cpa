Business Incorporation

Choosing the right business structure is one of the most important decisions you'll make as an entrepreneur. Grace CPA, PSC guides you through entity selection — LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp, or sole proprietorship — based on your tax situation, liability concerns, and long-term goals.

We handle the paperwork, coordinate with state agencies, and ensure your new entity is set up correctly from day one. A properly structured business protects your personal assets, minimizes your tax burden, and positions you for future growth.

From obtaining your EIN to making the right tax elections, we walk you through every step so you can launch with confidence and a solid financial foundation.

What's Included
  • Entity type selection & analysis
  • State filing & registration
  • EIN and initial setup
  • Tax election guidance
